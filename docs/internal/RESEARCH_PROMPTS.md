## PROMPT FOR RESEARCH:

```
I need detailed technical information about [COMPANY_NAME]'s document processing / OCR / IDP (Intelligent Document Processing) solution for the following analysis:

**Company Name**: [COMPANY_NAME]
**Context**: They are shortlisted for IndiaAI Challenge for government document processing (APAR and Disciplinary Cases)

Please research and provide the following information:

### 1. TECHNICAL PERFORMANCE METRICS
Find any publicly available benchmarks or performance data:
- **OCR Accuracy**: Character Error Rate (CER) or Word Error Rate (WER)
- **Extraction Accuracy**: F1 Score, Precision, Recall for key information extraction
- **Document Understanding**: Layout analysis accuracy (mIoU if available)
- **Summarization Quality**: ROUGE scores or BERTScore (if they do summarization)
- **Classification Accuracy**: Document type classification metrics
- **Processing Speed**: Time per page or per document
- **Supported Languages**: Number of languages supported
- **Maximum Document Size**: Page limits or file size limits

### 2. TECHNOLOGY STACK
- **OCR Engine**: What OCR technology do they use? (Tesseract, Google Vision, AWS Textract, custom, etc.)
- **AI/ML Models**: What models do they use? (GPT, BERT, custom LLMs, etc.)
- **Infrastructure**: Cloud-based or on-premise? Which cloud provider?
- **APIs Used**: Do they rely on third-party APIs?

### 3. PRICING & COST
- **Pricing Model**: Per page, per document, subscription, or custom?
- **Cost per Document**: Approximate cost (if available)
- **Cost per Page**: Approximate cost (if available)
- **Free Tier**: Do they offer a free tier?
- **Enterprise Pricing**: Any information on enterprise contracts?

### 4. FEATURES & CAPABILITIES
- **Document Types Supported**: What types of documents can they process?
- **Output Formats**: What formats do they generate? (JSON, XML, DOCX, PDF, etc.)
- **Batch Processing**: Can they process multiple documents at once?
- **API Availability**: Do they offer REST APIs?
- **Customization**: Can customers customize models or prompts?
- **On-Premise Deployment**: Do they offer on-premise solutions?

### 5. STRENGTHS & WEAKNESSES
Based on available information:
- **Key Strengths**: What are they known for?
- **Limitations**: What are their known limitations or weaknesses?
- **Unique Features**: What makes them different from competitors?
- **Customer Reviews**: Any notable customer feedback or case studies?

### 6. COMPANY INFORMATION
- **Founded**: Year founded
- **Headquarters**: Location
- **Team Size**: Number of employees (approximate)
- **Funding**: Total funding raised (if available)
- **Notable Clients**: Any government or enterprise clients mentioned?
- **Certifications**: Any security or compliance certifications?

### 7. RECENT DEVELOPMENTS
- **Latest Updates**: Any recent product launches or updates?
- **Partnerships**: Any notable partnerships or integrations?
- **Awards**: Any industry awards or recognition?

### 8. COMPETITIVE POSITIONING
- **Target Market**: Who are their primary customers?
- **Market Position**: Are they positioned as premium, mid-market, or budget?
- **Differentiators**: What do they claim as their competitive advantages?

Please provide:
1. Direct quotes from their website or documentation (with sources)
2. Links to any technical papers, benchmarks, or case studies
3. Comparison data if available
4. Note if information is not publicly available

Format the response in a structured way with clear sections and cite all sources.
```

1. **Use Multiple Sources**:
   - Company websites
   - LinkedIn company pages
   - Crunchbase

   - GitHub (if open-source)
   - Technical blogs
   - Case studies
   - News articles

2. **Look for**:
   - Technical documentation
   - API documentation
   - Pricing pages
   - Customer testimonials
   - Press releases
   - Research papers
   - Conference presentations

3. **If Data Not Available**:
   - Note it as "Not publicly available"
   - Make educated estimates based on similar companies
   - Focus on what IS available

4. **Verify Information**:
   - Cross-check multiple sources
   - Look for recent data (2024-2026)
